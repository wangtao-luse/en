9.形容词
   a.位置：位于be动词之后或名词之前;
    Paris is beautiful.
    London is expensive.
   b.可以使用副词really加强形容词的修饰程度;
      Brazil is really big.
      Salvador is really exciting.
    c.当形容词出现在单数名词前面时把冠词a或an置于形容词之前;(one of sth)，
       在以元音字母(a,e,i,o,u)发音开头的词之前用an;
      Salvador is an exciting city.
     Brazil is a big country.
  d.形容词的反义词
    exciting boring     wonderful    terrible
    quiet noisy		       big        small
    expensive cheap		       beautiful  ugly
